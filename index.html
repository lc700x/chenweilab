Welcome to Surface and Interface Lab !

Dr. Chen Wei's Surface and Interface Lab was established at the Department of Chemistry and Department of Physics, National University of Singapore, from Jan 2009. The current research interests of our lab include Molecular-scale Interface Engineering for Molecular, Organic and Graphene Electronics, and Interface-Controlled Nanocatalysis for Energy and Environmental Research. Our research has been substantially supported by NUS, Singapore MOE grants, NRF Investigatorship, CREATE and CRP programs, A*STAR and SPORE programs.
